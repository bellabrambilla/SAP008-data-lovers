C-137 Dimension

About

C-137, is one of the many universes in the multiverse and the universe where the mainstream Rick and Morty are often identified as Natives. Dimension C-137 is supposedly the universe in which Earth was invaded by Cronenbergs (Alien Race).
Inspiration: David Cronenberg (Canadian film director), is called by the press and the public as the "King of Venereal Horror" or "Blood Baron", Cronenberg is undoubtedly a master in horror, an area of ​​cinema that dominates as few, and that explores using mutations, parasites and diseases as "triggers", exploring dark environments and explicit visual references to the degradation of the body and flesh.
Curiosity: C-137 is also the name given to cesium-137, a radioactive isotope of cesium, which has a mass number (A) equal to 137, as it corresponds to the sum of the number of protons (55) and neutrons ( 82).
